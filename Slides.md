# EverBright GridSense — 5-Slide Executive Deck

---

## Slide 1 — Product Vision & Objectives
**Vision:** AI-powered home energy management that maximizes solar ROI and reduces bills.  
**Objectives (Year 1):**
- 15–25% avg bill reduction for users
- 30% attach rate on new solar installs
- 200K active users by Month 24

---

## Slide 2 — Market Opportunity & Target Segments
- **TAM:** ~4.5M U.S. solar + storage households by 2027
- **SAM:** 1.2M EverBright addressable customers
- **Primary users:** Solar + battery owners, EV households
- **Core JTBD:** Reduce energy cost without manual effort

---

## Slide 3 — Product Strategy (MVP → v2)
- **MVP:** monitoring, bill forecast, manual scheduling
- **v1:** AI auto-scheduling, TOU optimization, EV integration
- **v2:** demand response, VPP participation, predictive maintenance

**Differentiation:** hardware-native integration + AI savings engine

---

## Slide 4 — Pricing & Go-to-Market
**Pricing:** Freemium + $12.99/month premium automation  
**Channels:**
1. Bundle premium trial with new installs
2. Utility partnerships (TOU/DR)
3. Smart home/EV charger co-marketing

---

## Slide 5 — Success Metrics & Roadmap
**KPIs:** savings rate, automation adoption, premium conversion, retention, NRPU  
**AI:** forecasting, optimization, anomaly detection  
**Roadmap (18–36 months):** VPP marketplace, dynamic pricing, expand to non-EverBright systems
